The Product

For the Sharp Centre for Design 10th anniversary gala celebration, OCAD University was interested in commissioning an aesthetic data visualization installation. Working with co-designer David Schnittman, we designed a series of proposals, iterations and prototypes to arrive at a 120 minute video piece of data-driven ambient, motion design. The piece was a large scale video installation and served centrepiece for the celebration gala. One of the main goals of the piece was to convey information about OCAD University to its stakeholders in an accessible manner while maintaining and ambient and aesthetic manner.
