THE APPROACH

As the user interaction and interface designer, my role involved two different approaches. One was to define the visual style of the interface and brand of Wildflowers Mindfulness, and the other was to concentrate on creating user flows, wireframes and micro-interactions.

By completing competitive analyses, mood boards, concept sketches a visual brand language was established for the application. Concurrently, we worked on quick wireframes and a prototype of the application with main functionalities and interactions. Through a series of iterations and we refined our way to the release of the application into the app store.
